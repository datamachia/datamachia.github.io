About Me

Hi there! My name is Mel Bruno, and I’m thrilled to welcome you to my corner of the internet. Born in Brazil and based in London, I am an aspiring data analyst with a deep passion for uncovering the stories hidden within complex datasets.

With over 15 years of experience as a professional journalist, including four years dedicated to fact-checking, I have honed my skills in seeking the truth and presenting it in a compelling way. Recently, I decided to pivot my career towards data analysis, a field that has always fascinated me. I completed an intensive 12-week Data Analytics bootcamp, where I gained the technical skills and hands-on experience needed to dive deep into the world of data.

This space is where I share my journey, my insights and some of the exciting projects I’ve been working on. I believe in the power of data to drive informed decisions and create meaningful change, and I’m excited to connect with others who share this vision.

Let’s stay connected! Follow me on LinkedIn and GitHub to keep up with my latest projects and thoughts. Whether you have suggestions, are interested in collaborations, or just want to make a connection, I’d love to hear from you. Let’s explore the fascinating world of data together!
